RUTH - coursework by e-mail

This Grace Notes course makes it possible for you to study the book of RUTH, verse by verse, and receive a certificate for your efforts when you complete the course.

Each lesson consists of several e-mail files (1) the lesson instructions; (2) verse-by-verse notes on the passage being discussed; (3) two or more topical studies; and (4) the lesson Quiz. Some lessons will also include one or more files of optional supplementary reading.

When you register, we will send you the first lesson (see below for how to register). When you have studied the material, there is a quiz for you to complete and send back to Grace Notes. When we receive your completed lesson quiz, we will send you the next lesson. This will continue through the 10 lessons of the course. You will receive a certificate of completion after you send in the quiz for the final lesson. Note: a grade of 70% is required to pass each Quiz.

The Curriculum

There are ten (10) lesson in the RUTH course curriculum

Lesson 1 - Ruth 1:1-10
Topics Bethlehem; Moabites; Faith-Rest
Supplementary Reading Old Testament History; Rahab

Lesson 2 - Ruth 1:11-22
Topics Old Age; Depression; Worry; Peace
Supplementary Reading Gideon; Balaam

Lesson 3 - Ruth 2:1-7

Lesson 5 - Ruth 2:15-23
