The Gospel of Mark

This expositional Bible study of the Gospel of Mark was written by Dr. Dan Hill when he was the Pastor of Southwood Bible Church in Tulsa, Oklahoma. He taught the complete book, verse by verse, to his congregation there. He has made his notes available to Grace Notes for development into course materials.

Click to read, Right-Click to download the PDF of the complete study here: MARK
